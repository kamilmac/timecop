# Kumite Tournament Rules

## Registration
- Must prove martial arts mastery
- Invitation by previous champion only

## Fighting Styles Allowed
- Muay Thai
- Karate
- Kung Fu
- Sumo
- Capoeira

## Victory Conditions
- Knockout
- Submission
- Ring out


## Notable Champions
- Chong Li (1985-1987)
- Frank Dux (1988)
- Jackson (1989)
